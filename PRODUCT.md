# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Neighborhood residents seeking fresh, home-style daily meals (Thali, breakfast) without the hassle of cooking.

## Product Purpose
To sell daily/monthly subscription meals emphasizing hygiene, zero preservatives, and exact delivery times.

## Brand Personality
Brutalist clarity, concrete and punchy copywriting, premium, fast, confident.

## Anti-references
Typical AI SaaS templates (excessive glassmorphism, over-rounded corners, "ghost cards", identical 3-column layouts).

## Design Principles
- Practice what you preach: The design should reflect the "zero preservatives" ethos by being raw, unstyled, and authentic.
- Editorial layout over cards: Use space and typography to structure content, avoiding lazy borders.
- Concrete communication: Punchy, literal copy with no marketing fluff.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance for contrast. No small gray text on colored backgrounds. Avoid disorienting motion for those preferring reduced motion.
